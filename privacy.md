# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 18, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoPost Tool ("the App", "we", "our") collects,
uses, and protects information when you use our web application. By using the App,
you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account through TikTok's official Login Kit (OAuth),
we collect and store:
- **Basic profile information** (such as your TikTok display name and open ID),
  used to identify which account is connected.
- **Access tokens and refresh tokens** provided by TikTok, used to upload and
  publish content on your behalf.
- **Content you choose to upload** (video files, captions, hashtags, and scheduling
  details) for the purpose of posting to your TikTok account.

We do **not** collect or store your TikTok password.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth.
- Upload, schedule, and publish videos to your own TikTok account at your request.
- Display the status of your connected accounts and posts in the dashboard.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Access and refresh tokens are stored in encrypted form (AES-256).
- Data is stored locally in the App's database and is not shared externally.
- Uploaded video files are stored temporarily and may be deleted after publishing.

## 5. Data Sharing with TikTok

The App communicates only with TikTok's official APIs to perform the actions you
request. Your use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy
(https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your tokens and account data only as long as your account remains
connected to the App. When you disconnect an account or revoke access through your
TikTok settings, the associated tokens are deleted.

## 7. Your Rights

You may:
- Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok settings.
- Request deletion of your stored data by contacting us.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum
age required in your jurisdiction).

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the
top reflects the most recent changes.
